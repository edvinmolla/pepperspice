What are containers?

'Containers', are one of the fundamental technologies on and from which this platform has been built on.
They are merely miniservers, using less resources and providing greater reliability, scalability and lowering costs for everyone.

The number of Web Apps depends on the number of containers you have. One container can only have one Web App.
We try our best to help developers, thus we have created developing and testing ready containers which can be created easily and at very low costs.

Cfare jane kerpudhat?

'Kerpudhat', jane nje nga teknologjite themelore qe perdoren nga platforma jone.
Ato jane nje forme miniserveri, perdor me pak burime fizike, eshte me fleksibel dhe ul kostot per te gjithe klientet.

Numri i aplikacioneve lidhet me numrin e kerpudhave ne dispozicion. Nje kerpudhe mund te perdoret vetem nga nje aplikacion.
Ne perpiqemi te krijojme ambientin me te pershtatshem per zhvilluesit, prandaj, kemi krijuar paketa me kosto te ulet dhe shume lehte te krijueshme per fazat e zhvillimit dhe te testimit, duke u perpjekur te konservojme sa me shume kohen e zhvillueseve.
